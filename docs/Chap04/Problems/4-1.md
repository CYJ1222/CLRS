# 4-1 Recurrence examples

> Give asymptotically tight upper and lower bounds for $T(n)$ in each of the following algorithmic recurrences. Justify your answers.
>
> **a**. $T(n) = 2T(n/2)+n^3$.
>
> **b**. $T(n) = T(8n/11)+n$.
>
> **c**. $T(n) = 16T(n/4)+n^2$.
>
> **d**. $T(n)=4T(n/2)+n^2\lg n$.
>
> **e**. $T(n) = 8T(n/3)+n^2$.
>
> **f**. $T(n)=7T(n/2)+n^2\lg n$.
>
> **g**. $T(n)=2T(n/4)+\sqrt{n}$.
>
> **h**. $T(n)=T(n-2)+n^2$.
